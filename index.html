Incredible Things That Happen When You Do Yoga Every Day

  1. Better FLEXIBILITY AND STRENGTH.

    Yoga stretches your muscles. Upright postures work for the muscles in the lower body, and inversions strengthen the upper body.

  2. STRAIGHTEN YOUR BODY.

    Many yoga poses strengthen the core muscles of the back, which can help prevent back and neck problems.

  3. REDUCING LEVELS OF STRESS AND ANXIETY.

    Yoga includes breathing exercises. Deep breathing can help you relax, which can help relieve stress and anxiety.

  4. Reduce back pain.

    Practicing yoga can relieve chronic low back pain, help you function better, and enrich your quality of life.

  5. Improved sleep.

    Yoga is beneficial for sleep quality in the elderly, people with arthritis, pregnant women, and other groups.
